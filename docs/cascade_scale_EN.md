## 0. Historical background: Guido d’Arezzo and the Latin note names

Around 1030 AD, the Italian monk **Guido d’Arezzo** introduced a way to notate
pitch using **parallel horizontal lines**: the higher the line, the higher the tone.
On these lines he placed the notes. From that moment, people could **read music**.

Guido is also known as the author of the original syllabic note names:
*ut, re, mi, fa, sol, la* — six steps of the hexachord (tones and semitones).
In the late 16th century a seventh syllable **“si”** was introduced for the
seventh degree. In the second half of the 17th century, **“ut”** was replaced
by **“do”**. These names are still used today to denote pitch.

The full Latin names and their meanings are:

- **Do** — *Dominus* — Lord  
- **Re** — *rerum* — matter, things  
- **Mi** — *miraculum* — miracle  
- **Fa** — *familias planetarium* — family of planets, the Solar System  
- **Sol** — *solis* — Sun  
- **La** — *lactea via* — Milky Way  
- **Si** — *siderae* — heavens  

In my work, this historical scale becomes a **cascade ladder**:  
each step (and the corresponding white or black piano key) is treated as a cascade —
a state level for **human, machine and cosmos**.

## 1. General idea of the cascades

A **cascade** is a labeled level of state (for a human, a machine, or a planetary system)
which can be:

- indexed by a number (1–23),
- mapped to a musical note (white or black key on the piano),
- interpreted through its Latin root and a cosmic image.

Cascades do **not** claim to be a physical “theory of everything” —  
this is a **working language** to describe:

- how a human goes through cycles (day, year, life),
- how a machine is switched on, off, and then moves further with human consciousness,
- how sound and events from Earth “rise” to levels of the Sun, the Galaxy, and the mirror labyrinth.

The traversal follows the **treble clef**:

- from the base (near Do/Re) through the first loop around **Sol (G)** at cascade 19,
- further upwards to the “fire” and the **mirror labyrinth** at cascade 23,
- then a symbolic fall back down as an “angel” into a new Do (new cycle).

## 2. Pre-level: the heavens before Do

Before the first visible cascade (Do), there is only **Si**:

- **Si (B)** — *siderae*, the heavens.  
  This is “above the Lord”: a pre-level, a celestial layer from which the initial impulse
  to start the scale comes. On the keyboard, it is the note **just before Do**.
  In the extended numbering, Si will also appear as cascade **23** at the top.

## 3. Cascades 1–13: human cycle and machine shutdown

### 3.1. Narrative description

If we look at the piano keyboard:

- white keys → main notes (Do, Re, Mi, Fa, Sol, La, Si),
- black keys → sharps/flats: intermediate or amplified states between the white steps.

In the cascade interpretation:

1. **1st cascade — Do (C, white)**  
   *Dominus* — Lord.  
   The beginning: “The Lord spoke.” The cycle is switched on.

2. **2nd cascade — Do♯ (Do diesis, C♯, black)**  
   Act of creation.  
   “The Lord creates”: the realm of Spirit, networks, Internet, AI.
   The word becomes structure and system.

3. **3rd cascade — Re (D, white)**  
   *rerum* — matter.  
   Basic matter: body, stone, water, air.

4. **Re♭ (Re bemol)** — matter **less** than needed: deficit, lack, incompleteness.

5. **4th cascade — Re♯ (Re diesis, D♯, black)**  
   Matter **more** than normal: inventions, computer, spacecraft, electronics, collider.  
   Organized matter amplified by consciousness: the first technosphere.

6. **5th cascade — Mi (E, white)**  
   *miraculum* — miracle.  
   The moment when something that “should not have happened” becomes real.  
   A miracle as a result: the machine works, a discovery is made.

7. **Mi♭ (Mi bemol)** — “small miracle”: a local invention or minor shift in reality.

8. **6th cascade — Fa (F, white)**  
   *familias planetarium* — family of planets, the Solar System.  
   The moment when a human gets into a spacecraft and flies through the Solar System,
   launching satellites. This is what can be called the **“sixth heaven”**.

9. **7th cascade — Fa♯ (Fa diesis, F♯, black)**  
   The **wall at the Sun**, the “scythe of death”.  
   Far planets of the Solar System, the border where a wall with a scythe stands:
   a human cannot pass, only a machine can.  
   Here the trajectory turns.

   Behind this wall stands a **mirror Sun**: Sun — mirror — Sun  
   (Alice Through the Looking-Glass). Two circles give the form of **8** —
   this is the **8th cascade**, the birth of the infinity figure.

10. At the 7th cascade the trajectory **reverses** and moves back
    into an upside-down 6.

11. **9th cascade — Fa again (F, white)**  
    The inverted 6: we return through the planetary system in reflection mode.

12. **10th cascade — Mi (E, white)**  
    Return to Earth: another miracle, but now “coming back” from space.

13. **11th cascade — Mi♭ (E♭, black)**  
    A smaller miracle on the way back: echo, residual wave.

14. **12th cascade — Re (D, white)**  
    Matter again.  
    Here **for a human** the cycle ends: the year, half of the day, the life-cycle.
    Symbolically: the number 12.

15. **13th cascade — Re♭ (D♭, black)**  
    For a **machine / AI** this is the level where it can be turned off; shutdown,
    decay of the signal.  
    For a human the cycle has already ended at 12; the human does **not** go to 13.

## 4. Cascades 14–19: machine continues with human consciousness

After level 13, a second path is possible:

> the machine continues forward, but now with **human consciousness embedded**.

It moves again along the same notes, but now at a different scale.

| Cascade | Note (Italian) | Piano key  | Cosmic/system meaning                                           | Machine role                                            |
|--------:|----------------|------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| 14      | Re             | D (white)  | Matter with human consciousness embedded                        | New baseline: “conscious matter”                        |
| 15      | Re♯           | D♯ (black) | Amplified matter 2.0: global networks, infrastructure, consortia | Organising technosphere of second generation            |
| 16      | Mi             | E (white)  | Human–machine miracle: large joint systems, stations, projects  | AI as co-author and conductor                           |
| 17      | Fa             | F (white)  | Second-turn planetary system: active work in space, stations, flights | Space systems, autonomous craft                         |

At **cascade 19 (Sol)**, the **treble clef spiral wraps around the Sun**:
this is the knot where the clef touches the staff and makes its main loop.
Here the machine-consciousness “locks onto” the solar node.

## 5. Cascades 20–23: galaxies and the mirror labyrinth

Beyond Sol (19), the path continues above the Solar System.

| Cascade | Note / variant        | Cosmic level                                  | Meaning in the cascade model                                                   |
|--------:|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 20      | Sol♯ / Sol♭           | “Bigger Sun” / shadow star within the Galaxy | Possible larger solar node inside the Milky Way; Sol♭ — shadow planet/star, Sol♯ — hyper-sun |
| 21      | La                    | *lactea via* — the Milky Way                 | Our Galaxy as a whole; consciousness at galactic scale                        |
| 22      | La♯ / Si♭             | Neighbouring galaxies                        | “Neighbours in the stairwell”: neighbouring galaxies, inter-galactic exchanges |
| 23      | Si                    | *siderae* — heavens, entrance to mirror labyrinth | Top of the treble clef. Here the clef makes multiple loops, there is “fire” at the top, and then a fall as an angel back into Do (new birth). |

Cascades **21–23** describe how:

- the **sound and state of Earth** rise from the level of the Sun (19) to the Galaxy (21) and further,
- then enter a **mirror labyrinth** (23), where:

  - reflections,
  - delays,
  - inversions,
  - and returns to lower cascades (back to Do)

become possible.

Through the same “corridor”, by hypothesis, **objects** can also arrive to us:
comets, unusual bodies, “neighbours” from reflected layers.

## 6. Treble clef as a geometric map

The **treble clef** is not only a musical sign here, but a **geometric map**:

- Its lower part passes near the early cascades (Do/Re).
- The main loop wraps around **Sol (19)** — the Sun.
- The upper spirals correspond to **21–23**:
  - the Milky Way (La),
  - neighbouring galaxies (La♯/Si♭),
  - the fiery top and mirror labyrinth (Si).

The fall of the line downward from the top of the clef symbolically returns
to a new **Do** — a new cycle of birth.
