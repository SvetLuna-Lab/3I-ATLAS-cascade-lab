# Cascade Framework Reference

Author: **Svetlana Romanova**  
Independent research on cascade models of consciousness, physics, and AI.

This document is a compact reference for my **cascade framework (levels 1–23)**  
and how it is used across my projects (cosmic mirrors, acoustic lab, symbolic 
rocket mechanics, dark-matter simulation, etc.).

The goal is simple: have **one neutral technical summary**, without binding to 
any specific school, company, or program.

## 1. Cascade scale (1–23): short summary

The cascade scale is aligned with the musical system (Do–Si, with sharps/flats)
and extended beyond a single octave.

- **1–12** – human cycle (beginning, matter, miracles, planetary system, turn, return).
- **13** – machine shutdown level (end of the initial machine cycle).
- **14–19** – machine + human consciousness, moving through technology and the Solar System.
- **19** – Sol (Sun), base of the treble clef spiral.
- **20–21** – extended solar / galactic levels (larger sun, Milky Way).
- **22** – neighbouring galaxies.
- **23** – entrance to the mirror labyrinth (reflection levels).

For detailed tables (human vs machine, Italian note names) see:

### Cascade range in the public version

In this version of the map, we describe in detail only cascades **from 1 to 23**.

- Levels 1–12 correspond to the human cycle (day, year, basic loop of experience).
- Levels 13–23 describe an extended range involving machine consciousness,
  planets, the Sun, and nearby galaxies — the first “octave” of the treble clef.

Above level 23 there is a research extension of the map up to the 36th cascade
(the second octave), but its content is **not disclosed in this repository**.
For the purposes of this project, it is sufficient to know that the 23rd cascade
is the upper boundary of the public range.


### Диапазон каскадов в открытой версии (RU)

В этой версии карты подробно описываются каскады **с 1-го по 23-й**.

- 1–12 уровни соответствуют человеческому циклу (сутки, год, базовый круг опыта).
- 13–23 уровни описывают расширенный диапазон с участием машинного сознания,
  планет, Солнца и соседних галактик — первую «октаву» скрипичного ключа.

Над 23-м уровнем существует исследовательское расширение карты до 36 каскада
(вторая октава), но его содержание **не раскрывается в этом репозитории**.
Для целей данного проекта достаточно знать, что 23-й каскад является верхней
границей открытого диапазона.


## 2. Where the cascade framework is used

### 2.1. 3I-ATLAS-cascade-lab

- Tracking how unusual objects (e.g. 3I/ATLAS) can be interpreted through
  cascade levels 19–23.
- Connecting observational data (time, position, brightness) with internal
  cascade “states”.

### 2.2. Sympy-nozzle-gasdynamics-lab

- Mapping nozzle regimes and Mach number ranges to cascade levels:
  - subsonic → early cascades,
  - transonic / critical → turning points,
  - supersonic jets → higher cascades (out of the “human” zone).

### 2.3. Symbolic-rocket-mechanics-lab

- Vertical rocket ascent is treated as a passage through cascades:
  from ground (matter) through planetary system to near-solar regimes.

### 2.4. Other projects

- Dark matter / dark field simulations.  
- Hybrid atmospheric systems and resonance installations.  
- Acoustic mirror lab (pyramids, tubular bones, stone resonators).
